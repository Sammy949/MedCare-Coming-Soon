' MedCare Tips '

Night Outing

If going out at night, wear reflective or bright colored clothes at night and carry a touch with you. This will enable other road users to see you when crossing or walking on the street.

Traffic Rules

Motorists should obey all traffic rules, signs and regulations to avoid having accidents and risking both their lives and the lives of other road users.

Accidents at home

To prevent accidents at home, switch off any socket when not in use, dry wet floors, keep sharp objects out of the reach of children and most important, adapt good safety habits in the home.

Sports Safety

When having sports do not forget to free the play area from objects(dangerous) and people not participating in the sports. Also, use appropriate sporting kits or dressings for each sport they are used for.

More on Sport safety

Not to forget, Obey all rules and regulations governing each sport to enjoy a nice time and free your mind from stress. Do not forget warm-up activities precede any sport or game.
