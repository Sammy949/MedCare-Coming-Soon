'About MedCare'

The MedCare healthcare service app/WebApp was developed by SamY. MedCare is based on an idea of handy medical information and tools to assist daily living.


MedCare is equipped with a MedTerm dictionary which gives and breaks down medical meanings to understandable words which even an uneducated person can understand.


MedCare also has Safety tips and Health tips to keep you safe and in good shape. These tips are applicable in our day to day life and can even assist in saving someone's life or reducing damages/pains caused by injuries.
Not to forget, The MedNews, where infections and diseases active in the society will be explained to an understandable manner. It will include prevention methods, known available vaccines and more.


Stay Safe, Stay healthy. Caring for you is what we do.
